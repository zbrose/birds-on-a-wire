Welcome to Song Birds!

Song birds is a game designed to help you improve your ear for musical melodies.

The object of the game is to listen to a short melody and try to match it using your birds and their corresponding musical note.

    Here's how it works:
  1. Press the 'play/repeat melody button' and listen carefully to the melody.
  2. Then, click on your birds at the bottom of the screen to try and match the pitches!

You can try out a few combinations of notes using the 'QWERT' keys on your keyboard before answering.

You may also want to use the 'play C note' button as a place of reference for your budding ear. It'll help you if you get lost!

When you're ready, click on the wooden sign to start!
